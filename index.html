--- CONSENSUS-BASED VIRTUAL LEADER TRACKING ---

  • Virtual Leader: Swarm tracks a designated virtual leader node.
  • DDG Topology: Distributed Directed Graph for inter-UAV comms.
  • Consensus Control: Shared position data via consensus algorithms.
  • Push-and-Pull: Repulsive/attractive forces for collision avoidance.
  • Adaptive Steps: Dynamic step size for minimal transition errors.

Ref: Yildiz, B., Durdu, A., & Kayabasi, A. (2024). Consensus-based virtual leader tracking algorithm for flight formation control of swarm UAVs. [DOI]
