// Approach

Consensus-Based Virtual Leader Tracking

This project implements a consensus-based virtual leader tracking algorithm for swarm UAV coordination and formation control.

◎

Virtual Leader Tracking

Overall swarm guidance through a designated virtual leader that the UAV swarm coordinates to track and follow.

⬡

Distributed Directed Graph

DDG topology for structured inter-UAV communication, enabling efficient information flow across the swarm.

⟐

Consensus-Based Formation Control

Formation maintenance using shared position data and consensus algorithms across all UAVs.

⇄

Push-and-Pull Functions

Collision avoidance and formation keeping through repulsive and attractive force functions between agents.

Δ

Adaptive Step Size

Dynamic step size adjustment to minimize formation error during flight maneuvers and transitions.

Based On

Yildiz, B., Durdu, A., & Kayabasi, A. (2024). Consensus-based virtual leader tracking algorithm for flight formation control of swarm UAVs. Turkish Journal of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences, 32(2), 251–267. DOI →
